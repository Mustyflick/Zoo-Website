Unsere Tiere



Sid

A clumsy but good-natured sloth. Sid is often the comic relief, known for his childish antics and unfortunate situations.

Manfred

A woolly mammoth known for his grumpy and sarcastic personality. Despite his gruff exterior, he's loving, courageous, and protective of his herd.

Scrat

A frantic acorn-obsessed saber-toothed squirrel whose persistent pursuit of his acorn has comical yet often world-altering consequences.

Diego

A cunning and agile smilodon (saber-toothed tiger) who initially joins the herd with ulterior motives but eventually becomes a loyal friend.




Carl

A grumpy but resourceful groundsloth who often appears alongside Sid, frequently bickering with him.

Dodo

Dodo birds are extinct flightless birds that appear very rarely in the Ice Age, mostly as background characters

Zeke

This possum is also not a major characters but appear occasionally, often causing trouble for Sid

Brooke

A British ground s loth. Quirky and awkward, but endearingly so. Makes a grand entrance on a crystal hoverboard. She's confident and proud of her unique background.




Manny

A woolly mammoth known for his grumpy and sarcastic personality. Despite his gruff exterior, he's loving, courageous, and protective of his herd.

Buck

A wild and eccentric weasel with a missing eye who becomes Manny's unlikely ally. He's a skilled dinosaur hunter and adept at surviving in harsh environments.

Start

A hippopotamus encountered by Sid. While little is known about Start's personality or background, his presence alongside Sid adds a touch of the unexpected to the film.

Glyptodon

These massive creatures are walking tanks thanks to their thick bony plates, making them almost unstoppable predators.
